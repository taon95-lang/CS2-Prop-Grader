MATCH-LENGTH + PLAYER PROFILE FRAMEWORK
	1.	MATCH-LENGTH SENSITIVITY
Trigger when HLTV shows variance (odds gap, rank gap, stand-in, one-sided signals).

Build two projections:
Short-map → lower round environment
Normal-map → typical environment

Blowouts are not automatic Unders. Test both scenarios.

Key rule: can the player still clear in compressed rounds?
	•	Only clears normal → fragile Over
	•	Clears short → strong Over

2.	ROUND SWING (OPPORTUNITY)
Measures round impact and survival.

High: stable production, survives short maps
Low: drops in compressed maps

Improves efficiency, not total rounds.

3.	MULTI-KILL (CONVERSION)
Measures ability to convert into 2K/3K+ rounds.

High: strong ceiling, resilient in short maps
Low: needs longer maps, capped upside

Only upgrades Over if Round Swing is not weak.

4.	PLAYER PROFILE LOGIC
High Swing + High Multi → best Over
High Swing + Low Multi → stable but capped
Low Swing + High Multi → volatile
Low Swing + Low Multi → clear Under
