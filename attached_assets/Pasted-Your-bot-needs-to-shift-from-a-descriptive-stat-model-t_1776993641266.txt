Replace the simple "Last 10" average with a weighted system that prioritizes the last 5 matches (45% weight) while maintaining a 20-game baseline. This will allow the bot to capture genuine shifts in form while remaining grounded in the player’s statistical floor.

If a match has a heavy moneyline favorite, the "Projected Rounds" should be adjusted downward to reflect the high probability of a quick 2-0 or a low round count in regulation. The bot should not provide a final grade until the map veto is confirmed, or it should provide a "Conditional Grade" based on the three most likely maps. The massive performance delta between maps like Nuke and Dust 2 for players like Faven makes a "Map-Neutral" grade fundamentally flawed.

The "10/10" grade should require more than just high hit rates and historical averages.
